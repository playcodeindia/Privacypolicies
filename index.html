2. Countries We Serve

We primarily serve users from:

India Pakistan Bangladesh Sri Lanka

Japan South Korea England

Users from the USA and Mexico are served through separate domains and servers.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following user information:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address

info Important:

ID proof is collected only to verify age and identity and is stored securely in our high-end secure data centers.

4. Age Requirement

Our services are available to users aged 10 years and above.

If a user is found to be under the minimum age requirement, access to services may be denied.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use user information to:

  • Verify identity and age

  • Deliver redeem codes
  • Prevent fraud and misuse
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

6. Email Login System

We use an email-based login system.

Our system automatically checks whether an email address is already registered or not to prevent misuse.

7. Cookies

PLAYCODE OFFICIAL does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

8. Payments

Payments are made directly to our authorized agents via:

  • UPI
  • Cash
  • Other approved offline methods

warning Disclaimer:

We are not responsible for payments made to unauthorized or fake agents. Users must ensure they are dealing with official PLAYCODE OFFICIAL agents only.

9. Code Delivery

Redeem codes are delivered to the registered email address.

Delivery time:

  • Usually within 30 minutes
  • Maximum 24 hours, depending on server load

All codes are manually verified before delivery.

10. Invalid or Used Code Policy

If a code is found invalid or used:

  • A replacement code will be provided
  • Replacement is processed within 12 hours
  • No cash refund is provided; replacement code will be issued instead

11. Data Security

We use advanced security measures to protect user data, including:

However, no online system is 100% secure, and users share information at their own risk.
